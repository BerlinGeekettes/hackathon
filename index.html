March 1st, 2nd and 3rd - 2013

All the hacker ladies!

SoundCloud Women Developers organizes an All-Women Hackathon

clockwise

Hack Over 2 Days

24 hours of hacking will start Saturday at 1pm and go continuously (sleep optional!) until Sunday at 1pm. Our schedule starts Friday evening with Hackathon Prep School (for participants who want primers on technical and design topics) and ends Sunday afternoon with demos and prizes.

apple

Real Food, not Junk Food

How are you gonna survive all night building, coding, designing without fuel for your body? That’s why we’ll be providing tasty food that will make your body feel as good as possible so your fingers and brain can make awesome projects!

yoga

Relaxation and Yoga

You are going to need to take a break once in while. Or maybe you just need to walk away from your work and think. We’ll have places and to relax your mind and body.

All-Hacks Accepted

We know most people will do software hacks, but we also welcome hardware hacks, and any other category you want. This event is for everyone who want to builds a product or work on a project for 24 hours surrounded by like-minded people. Programmers, designers, people with just ideas and a lot of energy. This is your chance.

Why we have an application process

We want everyone to be successful, and for that we need a good blend of skills and talent. Everyone who wants to attend will be considered, but in order to have a great balance of people, we neeed to first review each person’s profile. We will begin sending acceptance notices on February 14th.

powered by OpenTechSchool

If you are new to Hackathons, using APIs, or are a new programmer, don’t let that stop you from applying! Let us know what you want help in. We’re preparing Prep School training sessions to be held in the evening on Friday, March 1st.
