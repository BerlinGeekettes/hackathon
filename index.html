All the hacker ladies!

The All-Women Hackathon

clockwise

Hack Over 2 Days

On Saturday at 1pm, 24 hours of hacking shall commence and end Sunday at 1pm – sleep optional! We will end the hackathon with demos and stellar prizes. Our schedule starts Friday evening with Hackathon Prep School for participants who want primers on technical and design topics.

apple

Real Food, not Junk Food

How are you gonna survive this all nighter of building, coding, and designing without fuel for your body? That’s why we provide healthy and tasty food that will make your body feel as good as possible so your fingers and brain can build awesome projects!

yoga

Relaxation and Exercises

You should take a break every once in while. Or maybe you just need to walk away from your work and think. We’ll have a designated relaxing area and a coach to help you stretch your muscles and reboot body and mind.

lightbulbon

All-Hacks Accepted

We know most ladies will do software hacks, but we also welcome hardware hacks, and any other category you want. This event is for all of you ladies ready to build a product or work on a project for 24 hours surrounded by like-minded people. Programmers, designers, people with just ideas and a lot of energy.

This is your chance.

compose

Our Application Process

We want everyone to be successful, and for that reason, we need a nice blend of skills and talent. Everybody who wants to attend will be considered, but to ensure a good balance of people, we first review every applicant's profile.

We will start sending acceptance notices on February 14th.
